Get to know about Align Dental

The team at Align Dental knows how important your smile is to you. We are here to keep you confidently smiling while keeping your teeth and gums healthy for life.

The philosophy of our practice is to provide you and your family with the highest standard of dental care and services in a warm, friendly and ambient environment.

Our team at Align Dental is dedicated to providing professional dental care to help improve your dental health and appearance. Whether it is emergency dental care, your regular check-up or aesthetic dentistry.

We offer a full range of dental techniques like braces, veneers, dental implants and teeth whitening.

We also know that not everyone is confident with visiting a dental clinic. We will always take time to listen to your concerns and offer you an understanding and sympathetic approach.

Meet Our Qualified Doctors

Dr. Christy Nayyar

MDS Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics

MDS Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics Dr. Nayyar is an Orthodontist at Align Dental Clinic. He completed his BDS in the year 2006 and did his Post Graduation MDS in the field of Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Bangalore in the year 2011. He is currently working as a Professor in the Department of Orthodontics at Himachal Dental College, H.P. and is associated with various hospitals and clinics as a Consultant Orthodontist. He is a dynamic doctor who excels in alignment of teeth and has a rich clinical experience in treatment of different types of dentofacial and skeletal malocclusions. He has attended various wokshops and seminars to hone his skills keeping in stride with all the recent advancements in his field of expertise. Dr. Nayyar has completed a certified course in Lingual Orthodontics and is a member of the Indian Orthodontic Society.

Dr. Shivika Ahluwalia

MDS Prosthodontics and Implantology

Dr. Shivika Ahluwalia is a Prosthodontist and Implantologist at Align Dental Clinic. She completed her BDS in 2014 and did her MDS in the field of Prosthodontics including crown & bridge and Implantology from Himachal Institute of Dental Sciences, Himachal Pradesh. She is soft spoken and a competent dentist. She has been awarded Distinction in her second year of undergraduate studies. Moreover, she is a distinction holder in Prosthodontics and was titled University Topper in the Himachal Pradesh University in her Post graduation studies in the year 2018. She has also been awarded the Best Presentation at a National CDE Program on Maxillofacial Prosthetics at Meerut. She has attended various national and international conferences in the field of general dentistry and Prosthodontics to update her knowledge and has presented various research papers and posters as well. She is also a lifetime member of the Indian Prosthodontic Society.
